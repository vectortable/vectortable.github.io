Privacy Policy


Last Updated Date August 3, 2020

The use of camera and microphone for recording:

  • If you use the screen, camera, or live sound recording via microphone features then you will be prompted for permissions as such.

Data Storage:

  • All your recordings will be saved in your device.

  • App does not collect, transmit, or save any data outside your device.

  • If you turn on the option to save your recording to Photos app location, you will prompted for as such permission.

Others:

  • When you save a recording, it will override the previous recording. You can prevent it by purchase the upgrade via in-app purchase.

  • This upgrade option will also allow you to save your recording to your Photos app location if so chosen.
