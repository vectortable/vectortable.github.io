Have you ever wished to have a mechanism to store all medicines informations and histories forever?
Have you ever wished to have a reminder for medication?
Have you ever wished to have a mechanism to create certain repeated activities and to store data related to those activities (Therapies, exercises, lab done, blood pressure measurements, weight, etc. for blood pressure and weight, a plotting charts can be viewed of data over time)?

You got it all with Health Cabinet app

The following features are included in Health Cabinet:

A.  A central place to hold personal information.
B.  A place to store your health care providers informations and location mappings.
C.  A place to store your insurers informations.
D.  A place to keep all medicines records, informations, histories, and to have access to it conveniently from a single device.
E.  A reminder for medication.
F.  A mechanism to set up repeated activities, saving note, and plotting data (Blood pressure and weight).  Activities can be set up with reminders.
G.  Activities data (blood pressure and weight) can be plotted in a chart format.
H.  A place to store all your related medical documents.  The supported document formats included Microsoft's Word, Excel, and PowerPoint, Apple iWork's Pages, Numbers, and Keynote, PDF, Rich Text Format (RTF), Images, Text (TXT), and Comma-separated value (CSV).
I.  Have access to the same data from multiple devices.  This feature required all devices signed in to iCloud using the same Apple ID and iCloud Drive enabled.

Health Cabinet is totally free for everyone for single user.  No ads involved.  For multi-user, it is an inexpensive IAP multi-user upgrade package.
